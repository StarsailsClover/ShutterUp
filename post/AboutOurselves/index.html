介绍我们自己

Tommy Wu

Tommy在中国广州成长，毕业于哈佛大学，是哈佛计算机科学系的全额奖学金得主。他凭借卓越的学术和研究成果，毕业时被哈佛聘为名誉教授，成为哈佛历史上最年轻的教授。

2018年，Tommy因其在科技和创新领域的突破性贡献，被《纽约时报》评为“年度最佳青年”。目前，Tommy担任BitQuantum公司的首席执行官，成功带领公司开发了爆款软件ShutterUp，彻底颠覆了行业格局。

Tommy以其卓越的远见和创新思维，持续推动BitQuantum在全球市场上的成功。

Sails Huang

🎨 视觉艺术设计师 & 技术开发者
🌐 职位：视觉艺术设计 | 前后端程序编辑

👋 大家好！我是团队中负责视觉艺术设计以及前后端程序开发的一员。我的设计风格深受 Apple、小米、Google、Microsoft 等大厂设计语言的启发，崇尚简约直观的用户体验。我熟练掌握主流编程语言（包括 Python、C、C#、C++、Java、HTML、VB、Rust、CSS、JavaScript 等），能够在设计与技术之间无缝切换，用代码实现美感。

✨ 团队精神
我一直认为团队是一个激发创意和增进交流的地方。这里没有孤立的个人，而是通过合作共同进步的伙伴。技术不只是冷冰冰的工具，它也能通过我们每一个人的努力，变得温暖、灵活且充满想象力。

🚀 开发经历
我的代表作品之一是 LightSearch，一款注重用户隐私且轻快流畅的聚合搜索引擎，采用类 Google 风格，支持用户自定义除底层代码以外的所有内容。此外，我也参与了 BetterNCM 和 BakaXL 4.0 的开发，用技术为更多用户带来便利与乐趣。

💡 期待
在这里，我希望用自己的专长为团队和用户创造更多惊喜与价值！一起打造未来吧！ 💻✨

Nick Li

Nick Li出生于中国广东省。他自幼展现出非凡的创造力和对知识的渴望。在剑桥大学完成本科学习后，他继续在该校攻读经济学硕士学位，并最终获得了经济学博士学位。Nick Li不仅在学术上取得了卓越成就，他还加入了一家在行业内享有盛誉的公司，该公司以其卓越的工作成果而著称。Nick Li不仅在商业领域取得了成功，他还是我们公司的创始人之一，他的创新思维和领导才能为公司带来了许多灵感和活力。他的远见卓识帮助公司在竞争激烈的市场中占据了一席之地。

Kevin Wen

Kevin在中国广州成长，毕业于香港大学，入学时凭借出色的成绩获得了全额奖学金。毕业后，Kevin加入微软营销部门，担任主管一职。在微软工作期间，他凭借卓越的表现获得了微软董事长亲自颁发的“年度最佳员工奖”。

目前，Kevin担任BitQuantum公司的首席营销官，充分发挥他的专业才能，推动公司在营销领域的战略增长与创新。在工作之余，Kevin热爱音乐，喜欢探索不同风格的音乐作品。

Jerry Luo

Jerry在中国广州成长，毕业于帝国理工学院，获得金融管理和计算机管理双学位，并荣获帝国理工全额奖学金。他以卓越的学术表现开启了多元化的职业生涯，曾在小米、苹果和微软等全球顶尖公司担任财务部主管。

在职业生涯中，Jerry帮助多家公司成功获得了大额投资，展现了卓越的财务才能和战略眼光。他的杰出贡献使他被《TheMarker》评为“最有前途的25岁以下人士”之一。

目前，Jerry担任BitQuantum公司的首席财务官，在推动公司财务战略和实现可持续发展方面发挥了关键作用。

Introducing Ourselves

Tommy Wu

Tommy grew up in Guangzhou, China, and is a graduate of Harvard University, where he earned a full scholarship in Computer Science. His remarkable academic and research achievements led to his appointment as an Honorary Professor at Harvard upon graduation, making him the youngest professor in the university’s history.

In 2018, Tommy was recognized by The New York Times as the “Youth of the Year” for his groundbreaking contributions to technology and innovation. Currently, Tommy serves as the Chief Executive Officer of BitQuantum, where he has successfully led the company to develop ShutterUp, a wildly popular software that has revolutionized the industry.

Tommy’s visionary leadership and innovative mindset continue to drive BitQuantum’s success on the global stage.

Sails Huang

🎨 Visual Art Designer & Technical Developer
🌐 Position: Visual Art Design | Front-End and Back-End Programming

👋 Hi everyone! I am a member of the team responsible for visual art design and front-end and back-end programming. My design style is deeply influenced by the design languages of major companies like Apple, Xiaomi, Google, and Microsoft, favoring a simple and intuitive user experience. I am proficient in mainstream programming languages (including Python, C, C#, C++, Java, HTML, VB, Rust, CSS, JavaScript, etc.), allowing me to seamlessly switch between design and technology, realizing beauty through code.

✨ Team Spirit
I have always believed that a team is a place to spark creativity and enhance communication. There are no isolated individuals here, only partners who grow together through collaboration. Technology is not just a cold tool; it can become warm, flexible, and full of imagination through our collective efforts.

🚀 Development Experience
One of my representative works is LightSearch, a privacy-focused and swift aggregated search engine with a Google-style interface, supporting user customization of all content except the underlying code. Besides, I have also participated in the development of BetterNCM and BakaXL 4.0, bringing convenience and joy to more users through technology.

💡 Expectations
Here, I wish to leverage my expertise to create more surprises and value for the team and users! Let's shape the future together! 💻✨

Nick Li

Nicolas was born in Guangdong, China. From a young age, he demonstrated exceptional creativity and a thirst for knowledge. After completing his undergraduate studies at the University of Cambridge, he continued his academic journey at the same institution, pursuing a Master's degree in Economics, and eventually earning a Ph.D. in the same field. Nicolas's academic achievements are nothing short of remarkable.Beyond his scholarly success, Nicolas joined a prestigious company renowned for its outstanding work in the industry. His innovative thinking and leadership skills have been a source of inspiration and vitality for the company. His vision has helped the company secure a strong position in a highly competitive market.

Kevin Wen

Kevin’s Professional Bio

Kevin grew up in Guangzhou, China, and is a distinguished graduate of The University of Hong Kong, where he earned a full scholarship upon admission. Following his academic success, Kevin embarked on a professional journey with Microsoft, where he excelled as a Marketing Supervisor. During his tenure, Kevin’s outstanding contributions were recognized at the highest level when he was honored with the “Employee of the Year” award by Microsoft’s Chairman.

Currently, Kevin serves as the Chief Marketing Officer at BitQuantum, leveraging his expertise to drive strategic growth and innovation in the company’s marketing initiatives. Outside of his professional achievements, Kevin is an avid music enthusiast who enjoys exploring different genres in his free time.

Jerry Luo

Jerry’s Professional Bio

Jerry grew up in Guangzhou, China, and is a graduate of Imperial College London, where he earned dual degrees in Financial Management and Computer Management with a full scholarship. His academic excellence paved the way for a dynamic career, during which he served as a Finance Supervisor at leading global companies such as Xiaomi, Apple, and Microsoft.

Throughout his career, Jerry has been instrumental in securing significant investments for the organizations he worked with, showcasing his financial acumen and strategic insight. His outstanding contributions earned him recognition from TheMarker, which named him among the “Most Promising Under-25s.”

Currently, Jerry serves as the Chief Financial Officer at BitQuantum, where he plays a pivotal role in driving the company’s financial strategy and ensuring sustainable growth.
