关于我们

ShutterUp APP 由 BitQuantum Technology（字节量子） Inc. 开发。
如您所见，这篇文章的封面是 知名科技公司 玖客云网络科技旗下的Nothingness Domain Studios（虚领工作室群） 的图标。这意味着我们是由强大的科技团队扶持而出的。

BitQuantum Tech. 是一家专注于数字媒体技术和社交网络解决方案的初创公司。自成立以来，我们一直致力于探索如何通过技术创新改善人们的日常生活方式。我们的团队汇聚了一群来自不同背景但拥有共同愿景的专业人士——即打造一个既能够激发创意又能促进人际联系的平台。

我们的愿景

作为一家云-AI优先科技公司，我们构建一个充满活力的社区，在这里每个人都可以自由表达自我、学习成长，并且从中获得价值。无论是寻求灵感、建立职业生涯还是简单地享受生活，快拍(ShutterUp)都将是你不可或缺的伙伴。

快拍(ShutterUp)不仅仅是一个应用程序，它代表了一种新的生活方式和社会互动模式。我们相信，在BitQuantum Tech.的努力下，这款应用将成为连接世界各地人们的桥梁，开启无限可能的新篇章。

我们的使命

  • 连接世界：让每一位用户都能轻松地找到并联系到他们感兴趣的人或事物。

  • 赋权创作者：通过提供强大的工具和支持，帮助内容创作者实现他们的梦想。

  • 推动行业发展：不断研究和开发新技术，引领数字媒体行业的进步。

About Us

The ShutterUp APP is developed by BitQuantum Technology (ByteQuantum) Inc.
As you can see from the cover image of this article, it features the logo of Nothingness Domain Studios (Virtual Leadership Studio Group), which is under the renowned technology company JiuKe Cloud Network Technology. This indicates that we are backed by a strong team of technology professionals.

BitQuantum Tech. is a startup focusing on digital media technology and social networking solutions. Since its inception, we have been dedicated to exploring how technological innovation can improve people's daily lives. Our team comprises professionals from diverse backgrounds who share a common vision—to create a platform that not only inspires creativity but also fosters interpersonal connections.

Our Vision

As a cloud-AI first technology company, we aim to build a vibrant community where everyone can freely express themselves, learn and grow, and derive value. Whether seeking inspiration, building a career, or simply enjoying life, ShutterUp will be an indispensable companion.

ShutterUp is more than just an application; it represents a new way of living and interacting socially. We believe that with the efforts of BitQuantum Tech., this app will become a bridge connecting people around the world, ushering in a new era of endless possibilities.

Our Mission

  • Connecting the World: Making it easy for every user to find and connect with people and things they are interested in.

  • Empowering Creators: Helping content creators achieve their dreams through powerful tools and support.

  • Advancing the Industry: Continuously researching and developing new technologies to lead the progress of the digital media industry.
